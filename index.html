Vision and Scientific Expertise

ChatSafetyAI is developed by leading researchers in AI and jobsite safety, with collectively over 200 peer-reviewed papers and 14k citations since 2005. Well before the AI boom, Dr. Antoine Tixier and Dr. Matt Hallowell led the way in applying machine learning to construction accident data.

Made by the Industry

The SafetyAI Council, a team of major companies, provide use cases to customize modes like Regulations, Photo HECA, and Pre-job Brief. They also share their accident datasets to train Super Predictive Models, boosting forecasts of risk and injury characteristics (Safer Together initiative).

Built for the Workplace

ChatSafetyAI features guardrails that block non-work-related queries, keeping usage focused and professional. It automatically blurs faces, redacts sensitive text, and never uses conversations for training. It can be deployed in-house for maximum privacy and security (on MS Azure or AWS).

Flexible & Expandable

Modes can be customized per company and accessed as APIs. ChatSafetyAI can have knowledge of your documentation, generate documents and presentations using your templates, and integrate with your systems.

Engineered Knowledge & Prompts

The SafetyAI R&D team curates cutting-edge safety knowledge and crafts precise prompts. This ensures ChatSafetyAI understands domain-specific jargon and provides reliable answers.

Best of Each LLM

ChatSafetyAI capitalizes on the respective strengths of multiple cutting-edge LLMs. This ensures optimized performance in image understanding, object detection, long context handling, and other specialized tasks.

SafetyAI Dashboard

The SafetyAI Dashboard is an interactive web application for analyzing and visualizing a company's incident and observation dataset (narratives + safety outcomes) using natural language processing, network analysis, time series analysis, and other data mining methods. The Dashboard also provides an interface to the SafetyAI predictive models (company custom and community), and to a binary and semantic narrative search engine. All the methods the Dashboard relies on were developed as part of multiyear research projects and have led to peer-reviewed publications in AI and construction journals and conferences. All methods have been developed and validated on large-scale, real-world incident datasets.

SafetyAI Assistant

The SafetyAI assistant is a simplified version of the SafetyAI Dashboard that features the functionalities that are the most useful onsite, with a user interface optimized for smartphones, and voice recognition capabilities. By answering a few simple questions about work conditions and the task at hand for a particular work period, the SafetyAI Assistant provides on-site personnel with: (i) a risk assessment, (ii) predictions of the most likely accident characteristics, should an incident occur, and (iii) historical injury cases that have occurred in similar work in the past. This information is very useful during, e.g., pre-job safety meetings.

Team

Dr. Antoine Tixier
Co-Founder, CEO

Antoine's PhD (2013-15) focused on how to predict the characteristics of construction accidents with machine learning. Between 2015 and 2020, while a postdoc researcher in deep learning and natural language processing, Antoine started to work on SafetyAI in his free time. He built on his PhD research to develop new incident data analysis and visualization techniques and better prediction methods, and created the SafetyAI Dashboard app.
In 2020, Antoine started working full-time on SafetyAI and created the SafetyAI Assistant app and the Safer Together approach (community predictive models). At the beginning of 2023, Antoine created ChatSafetyAI, the ChatGPT for OHSE. Antoine is in charge of every aspect of SafetyAI, from conducting research, training models and writing papers, to developing web apps, creating online content (tutorial videos, website), and interfacing with clients (demos, support, reporting, in-house deployment, admin and legal aspects)...

SafetyAI Council

Joining the SafetyAI council is the opportunity to participate in an industry R&D consortium with collaborative benefits: member companies are an integral part of our research and development process, by providing use cases, sharing accident datasets to improve injury prediction models (Safer Together approach), influencing research directions, and testing web apps in the field.
Updates from the most recent council meetings: Q1'24, Q2'24, Q3'24, Q4'24, Q1'25.

Safer Together Initiative: after anonymization and normalization, predictive models are trained on the aggregated datasets of the Council members. These community models (aka "Super Models") outperform the company-specific models at predicting injury characteristics most of the time, while also generating finer-grained forecasts. The SafetyAI Council's Super Models tackle the holy grail of safety cross-organizational learning and dissemination in the construction industry. Models are available in all of SafetyAI's web apps, and as an API.
