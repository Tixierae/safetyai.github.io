Vision and Scientific Expertise

ChatSafetyAI is developed by leading researchers in AI and construction safety, with collectively over 200 peer-reviewed papers and 14k citations since 2005. Long before the AI boom, Dr. Antoine Tixier and Dr. Matt Hallowell led the way in applying machine learning to construction safety data.

Made by the Industry

The SafetyAI Council, a team of major companies, provides use cases to customize modes like Regulations, Photo HECA, and Pre-job Brief. They also share incident datasets to train Super Predictive Models, boosting forecasts of risk and injury characteristics (Safer Together initiative).

Built for the Workplace

ChatSafetyAI features guardrails that block non-work-related queries, keeping usage focused and professional. It automatically blurs faces, redacts sensitive text, and never uses conversations for training. It can be deployed in-house for maximum privacy and security (on Azure or AWS).

Flexible & Expandable

Modes can be customized per company and accessed as APIs. ChatSafetyAI can have knowledge of your documentation, generate documents and presentations using your templates, and integrate with your systems.

Engineered Knowledge & Prompts

The SafetyAI R&D team curates cutting-edge construction safety knowledge and crafts precise prompts. This ensures ChatSafetyAI understands domain-specific jargon and provides reliable answers.

Best of Each LLM

ChatSafetyAI capitalizes on the respective strengths of multiple cutting-edge LLMs. This ensures optimized performance in image understanding, object detection, long context handling, and other specialized tasks.

SafetyAI Dashboard

The SafetyAI Dashboard is an interactive web application for analyzing and visualizing a company's incident and observation dataset (narratives + safety outcomes) using natural language processing, network analysis, time series analysis, and other data mining methods. The Dashboard also provides an interface to the SafetyAI predictive models (company custom and community), and to a binary and semantic narrative search engine. All the methods the Dashboard relies on were developed as part of multiyear research projects and have led to peer-reviewed publications in AI and construction journals and conferences. All methods have been developed and validated on large-scale, real-world incident datasets.

Team

Dr. Antoine Tixier
Co-Founder and CEO/CTO

Antoine's PhD focused on how to predict the characteristics of construction accidents with machine learning. Between 2015 and 2020, while a postdoc researcher in deep learning and natural language processing, Antoine started to work on SafetyAI in his free time. He built on his PhD research to develop new incident data analysis and visualization techniques and better prediction methods, and created the SafetyAI Dashboard app.
In 2020, Antoine started working full-time on SafetyAI and created the SafetyAI Assistant app and the Safer Together approach (community predictive models). At the beginning of 2023, Antoine created ChatSafetyAI, the ChatGPT for construction safety. Antoine is in charge of every aspect of SafetyAI, from conducting research, training models and writing papers, to developing web apps, creating online content (tutorial videos, website), and interfacing with clients (demos, support, reporting, in-house deployment, administrative and legal aspects)...

Dr. Matthew Hallowell
Co-Founder and Strategic Advisor

Matt is a President’s Teaching Scholar and Endowed Professor of Construction Engineering at the University of Colorado at Boulder. He earned a BS and MS in Civil Engineering and a PhD with a focus on Construction Engineering and Occupational Safety and Health. Before his academic career, he worked in construction as a laborer, project engineer, and quality inspector.
Matt specializes in construction safety research, with an emphasis on the science of safety. He has published over 100 peer-reviewed journal articles on energy-based hazard recognition, safety leading indicators, safety risk assessment, predictive analytics, and precursor analysis. For his research, he has received the National Science Foundation CAREER Award, the inaugural Thomas F. Farrell, II Safety Leadership and Innovation Award, and the Construction Industry Institute Outstanding Researcher Award.

SafetyAI Council

Joining the SafetyAI council is the opportunity to participate in an industry R&D consortium with collaborative benefits: member companies are an integral part of our research and development process, by providing use cases, sharing accident datasets to improve injury prediction models (Safer Together approach), influencing research directions, and testing models in the field.

Pricing Tiers

SafetyAI is entirely member-funded, with no external investors, to keep our research focused on long-term benefits, rather than on short-term profit. For this model to work, each organization needs to contribute proportionally to its capacity to support and benefit from innovation.
